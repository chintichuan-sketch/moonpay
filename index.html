Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about MoonPay

How do I buy cryptocurrency with MoonPay? +

To buy cryptocurrency with MoonPay, simply create an account, verify your identity, select the cryptocurrency you want to purchase, enter the amount, choose your payment method, and complete the transaction. Our platform supports various payment methods including credit/debit cards and bank transfers.

What is the transaction limit on MoonPay? +

Transaction limits vary based on your verification level and local regulations. Generally, unverified users have lower limits, while fully verified users can transact higher amounts. You can check your specific limits in the MoonPay app or website under your account settings.

How long does a transaction typically take? +

Most transactions are processed within minutes, but can sometimes take up to a few hours depending on network congestion and payment method. Bank transfers may take 1-3 business days. You can track your transaction status in real-time through your MoonPay dashboard.

What should I do if my transaction fails? +

If your transaction fails, first check your payment method to ensure it has sufficient funds. If the issue persists, contact our support team at +1-828-407-9844 or via email with your transaction ID for immediate assistance. We'll help identify the issue and guide you through the resolution process.

Is MoonPay available in my country? +

MoonPay is available in many countries worldwide. To check if our services are available in your specific location, visit our website or app and attempt to create an account. If MoonPay isn't available in your country yet, we're continuously expanding our services to new regions.
